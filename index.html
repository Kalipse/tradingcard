Red Fox

The red fox is the largest of the true foxes. Apart from its large size, the red fox is distinguished from other fox species by its ability to adapt quickly to new environments.

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes vulpes
  • Average Length: 45-90 cm
  • Average Mass: 2,2-14 kg
  • Average Lifespan In The Wild: 2-4 Years
